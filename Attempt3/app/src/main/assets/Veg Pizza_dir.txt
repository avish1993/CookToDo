Step 1

Mix the pizza sauce, garlic, garam masala powder, chaat masala powder, turmeric powder and lemon juice.
Step 2

Heat a pan with oil in it over moderate flame. Saute cauliflower, brinjals, carrots and onions in the pan.
Step 3

Spread the sauce mixture on the pizza base and keep aside some sauce mixture for the topping.
Step 4

Top up the pizza base with vegetables and tomatoes. Drizzle the remaining sauce over the toppings.
Step 5

Bake the pizza in a preheated oven for 10 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and crisp.
Step 6

Once the pizza is done, take it out from the oven and serve the dish nicely sliced.
